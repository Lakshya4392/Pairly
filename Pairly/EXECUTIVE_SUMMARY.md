## One-Line Pitch
**"Your partner's smile, always one glance away"** - A private photo-sharing app for couples with beautiful home screen widgets.

---

## The Problem
Couples struggle to maintain intimate visual connections in busy lives. Social media is too public, messaging apps lack presence, and existing couple apps are cluttered with unnecessary features.

## The Solution
Pairly enables couples to share instant photo moments that appear on beautiful home screen widgets, creating a constant visual connection throughout the day.

---

## Key Features

### Core (Free)
- Instant photo sharing between partners
- 1 beautiful home screen widget
- Real-time notifications
- Private gallery (50 photos)

### Premium ($4.99/month)
- 6 widget styles (Classic, Circle, Polaroid, Heart, Dual, Flip)
- Unlimited photo storage
- Shared notes with photos
- Time-locked photos (surprise reveals)
- Dual camera mode (front + back)
- Gallery lock (biometric)
