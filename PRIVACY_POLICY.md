# Privacy Policy for Pairly

**Last Updated: December 22, 2025**

Pairly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Data**: Email address, name, and profile picture (via Google Sign-In through Clerk)
- **User Content**: Photos and messages you share with your partner
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, and session duration
- **Push Notification Tokens**: For sending you notifications about new moments

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Pairly service
- Connect you with your partner
- Deliver photos and messages between partners
- Send push notifications about new moments
- Improve our app and develop new features
- Ensure the security of our service

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All photos are stored securely on **Cloudinary** (cloud storage)
- User data is stored on **MongoDB Atlas** with encryption at rest
- Authentication is handled by **Clerk** (secure third-party auth provider)
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell your personal information. We only share data with:
- **Your Partner**: Photos and messages you send are visible only to your connected partner
- **Service Providers**: Cloudinary (image storage), Clerk (authentication), MongoDB (database)

## 5. Data Retention

- Your account data is retained while your account is active
- Photos are stored until you delete them or delete your account
- Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your photos
- **Disconnect** from your partner at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **support@pairly.app**

## 7. Children's Privacy

Pairly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: support@pairly.app

---

By using Pairly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
